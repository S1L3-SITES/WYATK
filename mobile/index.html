Welcome to Luna Bugs Books!

We are sellers of Usborne books as well as organisers of events and competitions.

Many people may have heard of Usborne, many may not. You may even have some in your home!

They are known for a variety of book series' such as "Thats not my..." sensory touchy feely books for children as well as various lift-the-flap books and sticker dolly dressing.
