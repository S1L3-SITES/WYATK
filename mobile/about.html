About Luna Bugs Books!

Who am I?

My name is Lauren, and I am an Usborne Independant Organiser.

What does that mean?

It means that I stock and sell books, as well as organising events such
as facebook competitions and live broadcasts. Often these include my children.
They are Ophelia, who is 3 years old, and Luna who is 2 years old.

So it's named after your daughter?

Do you have to do much work?

It depends on how something was ordered. If it was through my selling page then it
is usually dispatched automatically. If someone has ordered something they have seen
that I have in stock, then I need to post it myself.

Who is Usborne?

Usborne are publishers of mainly childrens books.
They conceive and write almost all of thier children’s books in-house.
With a team of over 35 editor/writers, and over 50 designers, they can
publish over 300 distinctive and engaging books a year.

What kinds of books do they do?

Thier books range from playful baby books to young adult fiction,
and includes activity books, gift books, levelled reading books,
lift-the-flap information books, Sticker Dolly Dressing and
That’s not my… touchy-feely books. There is plenty of choice for
wide variety of age ranges!
