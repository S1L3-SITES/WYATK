If you are on this page, then you are wondering "what the hell is a hamburger menu?"

Since most websites are accessed using mobile platforms, tools are needed to make webpages fit better, look better and generally be "touch screen friendly"

To that end, you may have noticed that most website navigation is hidden off to the side, or under a menu with 3 dots, or 3 bars, they usually look like this:

These are the very bars that appear on this website, although these ones don't work, its just to demonstrate them to you. They are called a "hamburger" because they sort of ... look like one.

One click and it reveals the websites links usually with large finger-friendly spacing included.

Click the "hamburger" at the top of the page, and then click "Home" to get back to the homepage.
